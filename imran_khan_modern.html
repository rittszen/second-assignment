About

Imran Khan is a former Pakistani international cricketer and politician. He led Pakistan to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup and later became the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan (2018–2022).

Learn more on Wikipedia.

Career Highlights

1992 World Cup

PTI Founder

Prime Minister

Served as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan.
